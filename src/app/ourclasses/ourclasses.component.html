Our Classes

Bodybuilding

Bodybuilding is the practice of progressive resistance exercise to build, control, and develop one’s muscles via hypertrophy.

Weightlifting

Weightlifting involves lifting barbells competitively or as an exercise. It aims to build strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Yoga

Yoga, derived from the Sanskrit word “yoke” or “union,” aims to unite the body, mind, soul, and universal consciousness. It combines physical, mental, and spiritual practices.
